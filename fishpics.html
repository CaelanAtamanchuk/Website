Fishing Pictures

In the summer of 2020 the world shut down because of a pandemic. This led to me to go outside. Going outside led me to fall in love with the sport of fly fishing. Here are some pictures of the cool waters I've found and some of the fish that live in them. A special thanks goes out to all the fishing buddies I've picked up along the way who took a lot of the pictures below.
